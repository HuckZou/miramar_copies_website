About Miramar

Miramar Copies is affiliated with Miramar Group, which is one of the largest importer and wholeseller of new and used wide format, plotters, copiers ,scanners and their accessories which founded in 1999. Miramar Group has established a 3,000 square meters warehouse and a refurbish center in Nanjing. It cooperated with MITSUBISHI CHEMICAL in 2014 and became the agency of MITSUBISHI copier parts.

Miramar Group provides reliable office equipment. We have set up more than 30 branches around China including graphic shops and equipment selling companies. We have well-established service and technical network, with Nanjing as headquarter, Beijing and Guangzhou as two wings. We have established good relationships with the suppliers from the USA, Europe and Japan. Our goal is customer satisfaction, and we build strong and long term relationships by offering reliable products and services.
